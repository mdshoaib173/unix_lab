      pipe and filter commands

HEAD - This displays the head of the lines or data (when the number is mentioned)
FOR EG.
head -n 4 CSV_PROJECT.csv 


TAIL - This displays the head of the lines or data (when the number is mentioned) 
FOR EG.
tail -n 4 CSV_PROJECT.csv


CUT-This command can display any user specified column
FOR EG.
cut -d , -f 1 CSV_PROJECT.csv

SORT-It sorts the table according to the alphabets
FOR EG.
sort -d CSV_PROJECT.csv

WORD COUNT - It gives us a count with 3 blocks , i.e no. of lines , no. of words , no. of bytes.
FOR EG.
wc < CSV_PROJECT.csv
